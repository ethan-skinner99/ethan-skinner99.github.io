About Us

Rye High Academy lead by Master graduate students Ethan and Kenji was founded to enrich the current math currlimum. Sessions are desgined to be one-on-one focusing on the specfic needs for the student. From building upon elemtery math skill to undergrade university mathematics courses we offer a wide range of skill sets. We promise to help your confidence grow in order to reach your academic goals.
Rye High Academy, we provide one on one for high school mathematics(Grade 9-12), Calculus (grade 12) and university Calculus I, calculus II, Differential Equations, Probability and Statistics I, and Discrete Mathematics taught at all Ontario universities.
