Math Tutoring in Toronto

Ethan and Kenji the owners of "two tutors" are Math graduate students (Msc). We aim provide a very flexible tutoring service tailored to your child's needs. This can involve:
  • Ongoing and shorterm tutoring sessions
  • No commitments and no advance payments required
  • 30 minute sessions in addition to the typical 1 or 2 hour sessions.
We currently provide math tutoring at the highschool and university level. This includes
  • Highschool functions, advanced functions, calculus and vectors
  • University level Calculus, Discrete Mathematics, Statistics, Linear Algebra
  • Can also provide tutoring in programming for the languages Python and R

We promise to help grow your confidence in order to reach your academic goals. Please do not hesitate to contact us about any inquiries you may have at twotutorstoronto@gmail.com to see if we are a good fit for your tutoring needs.
