Shipping Information

We will only ship in reinforced corrugated tubes. We will NOT ship flat. Shipping flat only increases the chance of damage to the print. On the outside of the tube are handling and framing instructions. This also decreases the cost of shipping. Shipping costs: $25.00 USD, anywhere in North America. Outside of North America: Please contact us to receive an accurate shipping quote. If you are ordering two lithographs (one Richard and one Beliveau) they will be shipped separately to minimize any chance of damage. You will only be charged one shipping charge.

How to order

  1. Choose the Lithograph you would like to order.
  2. Choose the Number of the lithograph you would like. ( from under the picture)
  3. Click on the Ebay icon (This will take you to my ebay site. This allows you to have the Ebay purchase guarantee??)ll
  4. Just fill in your lithograph and number chosen, adding the price.
  5. Please read the shipping procedures under the shipping tab. Note: if you are ordering two prints, 1 Richard and 1 Beliveau, please read the information under the Shipping tab.
  6. Place your order
