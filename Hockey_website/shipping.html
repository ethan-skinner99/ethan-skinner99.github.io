Safe & Secure Delivery

Shipping Information

We ship exclusively in reinforced corrugated tubes — we will NOT ship flat. Flat shipping only increases the chance of damage to these valuable prints.

Each tube includes handling and framing instructions on the exterior, and this method also reduces shipping costs.

Shipping Rates

$25 USD — anywhere in North America

Outside of North America: Contact us for an accurate shipping quote.

Note: If you order two lithographs (one Richard and one Beliveau), they will be shipped separately to minimize any chance of damage. You will only be charged one shipping fee.
