THE GOALS NUMBERING SYSTEM

Maurice Richard scored 544 career goals and 82 playoff goals during his NHL career. Commemorative Goals is offering one signed print for each goal scored. Therefore, each print is unique and represents one of his 626 NHL goals. All prints in this series have been autograph authenticated by PSA — "The leader in third party signature verification." In addition, there are 63 signed artist proofs.
